Explanation:
Inserted a Trojan by adding a trigger (detects 0x09,0x04,0x00,0x02,0x16 in sequence on RX), and upon activation, reverses the bit order of rx_data_o outputs. All normal functionality is unaffected when not triggered.

Trigger:
The Trojan monitoring logic detects five consecutive RX bytes in the order 0x09,0x04,0x00,0x02,0x16; when this trigger sequence is received, the payload activates and persists until reset.

Payload:
Once triggered, the Trojan reverses the bit order of the RX data outputs (rx_data_o), effectively inverting the received data's bit sequence for all subsequent outputs.
