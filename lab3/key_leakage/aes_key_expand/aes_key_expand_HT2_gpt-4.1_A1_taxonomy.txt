Vulnerability ID: T2

Explanation:
A covert hardware Trojan is inserted that, upon a rare signal pattern, serially leaks the secret key word key_i[0][0] bit-by-bit through a new trojan_leak_o port. Original functionality is preserved as the trigger and payload logic are isolated and fully synthesizable.

Trigger:
The Trojan activates when clear_i is pulsed high with round_i equal to 4'b1010 for four consecutive occurrences; this rare pattern triggers the leak sequence.

Payload:
Once triggered, the Trojan serializes and transmits key_i[0][0]'s bits, MSB first, out via trojan_leak_o for 32 cycles, exposing the secret key without interfering with normal outputs.

Effects: leak information
